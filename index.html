About Me

Howdy! I am Kayla Bui, a first generation college student from the Metropolitan city of Houston Texas. Currently, I am pursuing my education at Texas A&M University College Station and I am a Fightin' Texas Aggie Class of 2026. Originally, I was an architecture major, but I did not enjoy the coursework and environment because it did not align with my passions, so I set my eyes on business. I am currently pursuing my education in the Bachelor's of Science in Business Administration with a specialized minor.

While I may lean towards introversion, I can be quite the extrovert when people get to know me. Besides academia, my hobbies include drawing, painting, hanging out with friends, and shopping. In addition, I am involved with the Asian Business Society and a few other organizations on campus to foster a learning community. I am the elder of two siblings and I look forward to leading my sister to college, preferably, Texas A&M and we could begin a tradition of being an Aggie Family!

You might be thinking why I have created this website right? I have developed this website for my Fundamentals of Information Systems class which is a testament of me trying out new things.
